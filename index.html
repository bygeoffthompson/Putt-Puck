Putt Puck

A golf ball alternative designed for golfers to practice their putting on smooth hard surfaces at home.

Putting practice no longer requires a green felt mat - or even carpet!

Putt Puck slides over smooth surfaces and opens up new areas of the house to practice:

Bamboo

Hardwood

Laminate

Linoleum

Tile

Vinyl

and more!

The Putt Puck and hard surfaces have several practice benefits over putting balls on carpet or felt:

  • Hard surfaces are more level than products laid on top of them.
  • Golf ball putts are effected by soft surfaces' variations. Putt Puck results are solely the outcome of the putter's face angle and path.
