FAQs

Putt Puck slides across a variety of surfaces with a slick and smooth plastic lower layer.

A low center of gravity prevents Putt Puck from toppling over. This allows golfers to strike their putts as they normally would on the course.

Putt Puck weighs 33% less (31g) than a regulation golf ball (46g). This allows it to effortlessly slide for long distances.
