Putting Puck

A golf ball alternative designed for putting on smooth hard surfaces.

Putting practice no longer requires carpet or an ugly green felt mat.

The Putting Puck is a golf ball alternative that slides over rigid household surfaces. This opens up new areas of the house to practice your putting:

  • Bamboo
  • Concrete
  • Hardwood
  • Laminate
  • Linoleum
  • Tile (Ceramic, Porcelain, Stone)
  • Vinyl

Putting with the Putting Puck on hard surfaces has several benefits over putting balls on carpet or felt:

  • Hard surfaces are more level than products laid on top of them.
  • Putts are not effected by soft surfaces' variations. Putt results are solely the outcome of your putter's face angle and path.
  • The puck's design increases friction and ingrains a putting stroke that accelerates through the ball.
