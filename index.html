FAQs

Great!

Putt Puck utilizes a slick and smooth plastic lower layer to slide across smooth surfaces.

Its weight (31g) is just 2/3rds of a regulation golf ball (46g). This allows Putt Puck to travel effortlessly for a variety of lengths.

Real!

Putt Puck's entire striking surface is a regulation size urethane golf ball.

It weighs less than regulation to help offset sliding's increased friction and provide realistic run.
